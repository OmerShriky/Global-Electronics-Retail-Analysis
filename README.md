# Global-Electronics-Retail-Analysis
Global retail dashboard analyzing profitability, currency fluctuations, and market expansion opportunities.

Conclusions:

1) Physical retail remains the primary revenue driver, particularly in the US market, which accounts for the largest share of global sales. The Adventure Works Desktop has emerged as a flagship product, significantly outperforming other categories in high-volume regions.

2) Geographic analysis reveals high-potential "Opportunity Gaps" in Munich, Paris, and Sydney, where high online sales volume indicates strong demand despite the absence of a physical presence. Conversely, markets like the UK, Italy, and Vancouver show lower engagement, suggesting a need for localized marketing or a review of regional product-market fit.

3) Temporal analysis confirms a sharp decline in physical store performance starting in March 2020, aligned with global pandemic restrictions. While online channels showed resilience, the overall recovery trajectory for brick-and-mortar locations remains gradual, highlighting a shift in consumer behavior toward a "Digital-First" model.
